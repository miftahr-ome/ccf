ABOUT US

Care & Compassion (BD) is a non-profit foundation

Our Mission

To uplift lives by offering holistic care, enabling self-reliance, and delivering hope to those who need it most.

Our Vision

A Bangladesh where every person, regardless of their background, has access to essential services, education, and a dignified life.

What We Do & Values

We believe in compassion, transparency, accountability, and respect. Our work spans multiple focus areas:

  • Health and Nutrition
  • Education and Skills Training
  • Emergency Assistance and Humanitarian Relief
  • Community Empowerment

How We Operate

We ensure transparent operations; we monitor outcomes; we prioritize community participation; we allocate funds responsibly.
